About Us

I am a front-end developer with a passion for helping local businesses improve their online presence. I believe that every business deserves a website that is both beautiful and functional, and I am committed to helping businesses achieve their goals through technologies.

I have a Computer Science Bachelor and over 5 years of experience in the web development industry. I have worked with a variety of businesses. I am proficient in a variety of web development technologies, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, SQL, PHP and WordPress.

Besides my front-end developer skills I worked for a company for over 5 years improving Search Engine Optimization (SEO) and managing online platforms such as Amazon, Ebay, Overstock and Walmart. I developed strategies and campaigns that reached $40k weekly in sales.

If you are a local business owner who is looking to improve your online presence, I would be happy to discuss your web design needs. I would love to help you create a website that will help you reach your target audience and achieve your business goals.
